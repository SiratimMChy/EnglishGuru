Professional Phonetic Tips

The Schwa Sound /ə/

Most common vowel in English. Practice: about, problem, system, professional

Word Stress Patterns

Stress first syllable in nouns: PROject, CONtract. Stress second in verbs: proJECT, conTRACT

Linking Sounds

Connect words smoothly: "an apple" → "anapple", "first time" → "firstime"

Intonation Patterns

Rising for questions, falling for statements. Practice: "Really?" vs "Really."
